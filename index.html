Watermelon Cooler Slushie

Watermelon cooler slushie, easy to make and fun to share with friends on a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • ⅓ cup fresh lime juice
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Method

  1. Place watermelon and ice into a blender
  2. Pour in lime juice, sugar, and salt into the blender
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Dip the rim of glass in lime and salt, pour contents into cup and enjoy!
