Kentucky Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped carrots
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 1 khara bi tizeh

Method

  1. melt the butter and kol khara
  2. Put in the ligma and suck my tiddies
  3. Pour the hot soup into a blender, filling the pitcher no more than halfway full
  4. Serve hot
